Are you also an upcoming student who wants to have conversations with Humber student ambassadors or read about their campus experiences? This website has got you covered.

Humber College has appointed a select group of student ambassadors to foster effective communication between current students and incoming ones. These ambassadors provide guidance, respond to inquiries, and share their campus experiences to help new students transition smoothly into college life. This initiative by Humber is a commendable step toward enhancing student engagement and support.

To further simplify the process, I developed this website to facilitate direct access to these ambassadors, enabling prospective students to easily connect, seek assistance, and gain insights from their experiences.
